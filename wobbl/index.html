Wobbl

Wobbl is a balance board, a versatile, open-ended gross motor toy for 3-5 year olds to learn and grow with!


It has several uses for physical and mental health development and endless possibilities for open-ended play among children.


The board helps children exercise indoors. When they wobble with the board they work out muscles on the body that usually are not worked out otherwise at this tender age!


It strengthens the core and ankles. It is a perfect indoor toy with many different ways to be played with.


Using a balancing board also helps children understand the space around them and control their body accordingly.


An image of Wobbl

How To Use Wobbl


To make the best use of Wobbl, a child must step onto it with the help of a guardian, and wobble from side to side, trying to maintain balance.


The rocking movement will help in sensory development.


Wobble boards are usually gently curved. Wobbl, on the other hand, is flat, with a hemisphere placed at the bottom, so that children do not risk breaking it or hurting themselves if they use the toy in the absence of a guardian.



A snapshot of the CAD of of Wobbl


Wobbl was designed using Solidworks, and has dimensions of
22.0 x 8.0 x 0.75.

It is ideal for use by 3-5 years old, but can comfortably hold more than 135lb.

It has been built by rounding out the edges of a wooden plank and connecting it to a half-cut PVC pipe (a hemisphere) to create the wobbling effect.
